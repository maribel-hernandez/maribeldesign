Sabor Oaxaca

For one of my educational projects, I was assigned to find a local Los Angeles food truck that needed help rebranding their business. As a result I chose a Oaxacan Cuisine food truck, Oaxaca on Wheels, and rebranded them to Sabor Oaxaca. It was important for me to translate their extensive food menu, but simplify their branding. In addition, I wanted this rebrand to help distinguish them by ensuring I kept intact the traditional, homestyle feel that highlights their cuisine. From deciding on their brand colors to creating various design assets, it was important to stay consistent in all marketing materials.
