Mezcal El Padrino

Mezcal El Padrino was designed and created for a class assignment. Our task was to design a label for an alcohol bottle, then create a magazine ad to promote the bottle. As a result, I decided to design a label for a mezcal brand from Oaxaca, Mexico. I used Adobe Illustrator to design the label artwork and Adobe Photoshop to design the magazine ad. My goal with the label was to transmit a feeling of richness in flavor and culture, while ensuring the bottle design followed FTC Guidelines.
