Arrazola Designs

Arrazola Designs was inspired by my sister and I’s love for design and creativity. Proudly based in Los Angeles, California, we decided to embark on a new venture of sharing our passions with the rest of the world. Combining both our work experiences and educational background, we knew that creativity would be the focus of our business. As a result, I designed our brand logo, developed our website, and run our online presence. We our continuing to evolve our services from websites to personalized invitations and as a result, our brand will too.
